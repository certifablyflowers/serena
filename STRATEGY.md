## Instagram-Specific Growth Tactics

### Pattern Break Architect
Prompt: "Act as a senior Instagram growth strategist. Analyze the nursing/healthcare niche and identify overused content patterns. Create 10 post ideas that break those patterns while still fitting Instagram's algorithm. Each idea must feel unexpected and scroll-stopping."

### Hook That Hijacks Attention
Prompt: "Act as a viral copywriter. Rewrite this idea into 5 brutally strong opening hooks designed to stop scrolling instantly. Each hook must create curiosity, tension, or disbelief without clickbait."

### Silent Content Multiplier
Prompt: "Act as a faceless content expert. Convert this idea into a text-only or visual-only post that requires no talking, no face, and no trends. Optimize for saves, shares, and re-reads."

### Algorithm-Friendly Rewrite
Prompt: "Act as an Instagram algorithm analyst. Rewrite this post to maximize watch time, completion rate, and saves. Structure the copy so each line pulls the reader to the next."

### Scroll Retention Engineer
Prompt: "Act as a retention specialist. Break this post into a line-by-line sequence that forces readers to keep scrolling. Each line should slightly increase curiosity or value."

### Repurpose Everywhere
Prompt: "Act as a content repurposing strategist. Turn this single post into variations that feel native to Instagram feed, Reels captions, TikTok, and X without changing the core idea."

### Invisible Authority Builder
Prompt: "Act as a brand positioning expert. Rewrite my content so it subtly positions Brookey as someone who knows what they're doing without bragging, flexing numbers, or sounding like a guru. The goal is trust, not hype."
